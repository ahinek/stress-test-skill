# Example: Jira Workspace Consolidation Stress Test Generated Prompt

Prompt:

Stress test this operational change initiative:

Plan:
We are consolidating several independent Jira workspaces into a single centralized Jira workspace.

The goals of the consolidation are:
- central visibility into all work
- standardized workflows and issue types
- improved reporting consistency
- easier cross-team dependency management
- shared operational practices
- reduced administrative overhead
- improved portfolio-level planning and prioritization

The initiative includes:
- migrating existing projects into the centralized workspace
- standardizing workflows, fields, and issue types
- consolidating dashboards and reporting
- redefining permissions and ownership models
- introducing shared governance standards
- retraining teams on the new structure and expectations

Audience / stakeholders:
Engineering teams, product managers, scrum masters, release management, quality engineering, portfolio leadership, Jira administrators, and executive leadership.

Success criteria:
- all active work tracked in a centralized workspace
- improved visibility across teams and initiatives
- more consistent reporting and metrics
- reduced duplication and fragmented workflows
- improved dependency coordination
- adoption of shared operational standards
- minimal disruption to delivery work during migration

Constraints:
- teams currently use Jira differently across organizations
- workflows and field definitions are inconsistent
- some teams have highly customized boards and automations
- delivery commitments remain active during migration
- reporting expectations from leadership will continue uninterrupted
- Jira administration capacity is limited
- historical data quality is inconsistent

Known concerns:
- teams may resist standardized workflows
- operational disruption during migration may reduce delivery throughput
- existing automation and integrations may break
- centralized governance may create bottlenecks
- teams may create shadow processes outside Jira
- reporting consistency may improve superficially while underlying practices remain inconsistent
- local team needs may be lost in the push for standardization

Please provide:
- Most likely failure
- Most dangerous failure
- Cheapest failure to prevent
- Hidden assumption
- Base-rate reasoning
- Organizational failure mode analysis
- Revised plan recommendations
- Pre-launch checklist
- Early warning indicators
