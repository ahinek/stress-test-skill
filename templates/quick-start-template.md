# Stress Test Quick Start Template

Copy, paste, and complete the sections below.

```text
Stress test this:

Plan:
[What are we planning to do?]

Audience / stakeholders:
[Who is affected, who needs to support this, and who may resist it?]

Success criteria:
[What would make this successful?]

Constraints:
[Deadlines, staffing, technical limitations, budget limits, political realities, dependency constraints, or quality expectations.]

Known concerns:
[What already feels risky, unclear, controversial, overloaded, unrealistic, or assumed?]
```

The stress-test skill will analyze for:
- likely failure modes
- organizational risks
- hidden assumptions
- coordination breakdowns
- dependency problems
- unrealistic expectations
- AI amplification risks
- prevention opportunities
- early warning indicators
